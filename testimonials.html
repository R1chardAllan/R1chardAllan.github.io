Testimonials

Major Russell A. Hill, United States Marine Corps

I had the privilege of working with and observing Richard Allan as he excelled in numerous Career Recruiter billets for Recruiting Station (RS) Kansas City. As the Commanding Officer of RS Kansas City, I got to know Richard as a Marine who is steadfast, enthusiastic, and reliable. He is a forward-thinking person, who thrives on and welcomes new challenges, showing resilience in the face of hardship, and has a tremendous ability to solve unique problems. The best examples of his leadership came in his ability to train the members of our team, using his robust technical knowledge to build skill and confidence within each Marine around him. His initiative, outstanding leadership, effective communication skills, and high moral character are a force multiplier to any organization.
